About our President

Jeon Soyeon is the 7th President of Sunbic University. A Leader, Teacher, Singer, and Rapper, President Soyeon brings the all around global vision to Sunbic in creating a fair society.

In order to create that "fair" society, Soyeon plans to immerse herself in cultural life
of Sunbic, by meeting, listening, and forging connections around Sunbic Community.


Jeon Soyeon believes that educations and commitments to equity and justice are very essential in transforming our society. Soyeon has more than half decade of higher education experience. During that time, she has been helping to envision, define, and achieve greater outcomes for students, faculty, and staff. As a leader, her works has ensured that institutions are more effective, mission-aligned and diverse, equitable and inclusive.

Prior to her work at Sunbic University, Soyeon was having her solo debut as a trainee in January 2016, she ranked at number 20 on the last episode and failed to become a member of the winning girl-group. In July 2016, she appeared as a contestant in a rap competition show Unpretty Rapstar. The show finished with Soyeon as the second runner-up. In December 2016, she signed an exclusive contract as an artist under the agency of Cube Entertainment. She officially debuted on June 2017, with two songs that she wrote herself as the digital singles. On January 2018, it was announced that she finished her contract in Cube Entertainment and decided to change her career in the education field. She became a vice president of Cibnus University in late April 2018 (2018-2019). In November 2019 she became the President as we know in Sunbic University (2019-2025).
